Our Family Services

Comprehensive support tailored to your family's unique needs

Parenting Support

Workshops, coaching, and resources to help you navigate every stage of your child's development.

  • Positive discipline
  • Child development guidance
  • Special needs support

Family Counseling

Professional therapy for families facing conflict, grief, or major life transitions.

  • Conflict resolution
  • Grief and loss
  • Blended family support

Early Childhood

Developmental screenings, playgroups, and early intervention programs for children 0–5.

  • Developmental assessments
  • Parent-child playgroups
  • Kindergarten readiness

Family Resource Navigation

Connect families with community resources: housing, food assistance, healthcare, and more.

  • Basic needs assistance
  • Healthcare referrals
  • Legal aid connections

Mental Health Support

Individual and group therapy for parents, children, and teens facing anxiety, depression, or trauma.

  • Individual therapy
  • Support groups
  • Trauma-informed care

Respite Care

Temporary relief for primary caregivers of children with special needs or chronic conditions.

  • In-home respite
  • Weekend programs
  • Sitter training

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of social workers and parents, Harmony Family Services began as a small support group and grew into a trusted community resource.

We believe every family deserves compassionate, culturally responsive support. Our team works alongside you to build on your strengths and navigate challenges with dignity and respect.
