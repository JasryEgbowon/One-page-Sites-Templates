# One-Page Portfolios · 10 Industry Templates

A collection of clean, responsive one-page websites built for specific business needs. No frameworks, no bloat - just solid HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS.

## What's This For?

Quick starter templates for client work. Each one is tailored to a specific industry with the right content structure and vibe out of the box.

## The Templates

**Tech & SaaS**  
- Modern SaaS → Clean layout with features grid and pricing tiers

**Creative & Retail**  
- Design Agency → Bold portfolio grid and team showcase  
- Fashion Boutique → Minimalist lookbook and product focus  
- Coffee Roastery → Warm, rustic feel with product highlights

**Services**  
- Legal Firm → Trust-focused with practice areas and credentials  
- Fitness Coach → High-energy with programs and success stories  
- Eco Consultant → Earth-toned with impact metrics and mission

**Specialty**  
- Aviation School → Professional training program showcase  
- Childcare Center → Bright, friendly layout for parents  
- Independent Artist → Immersive portfolio with media integration
